# Star Ranker — Terms of Oracle Service & Jurisdictional Compliance

## 1. Nature of the Platform
Star Ranker is a cultural ranking platform. "Staking" on assets represents a vote of confidence in the future popularity or reputation of an item. It is NOT an investment, derivative, or financial instrument.

## 2. Beta Testing Status
This platform is currently in **BETA**. All items, scores, and balances are subject to reset. No real-world currency is currently involved unless explicitly stated in a secured fiat portal.

## 3. Jurisdictional Gating
By using this platform, you acknowledge that:
- You are not a citizen or resident of a sanctioned region (Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Russia).
- You are complying with your local laws regarding crypto-assets and speculative rankings.
- In certain regions, "Stake" functions are disabled and replaced with "Free Reputation Voting".

## 4. Risks
- **Oracle Velocity Dampening**: High-frequency voting may trigger dampening algorithms.
- **Epoch Reification**: System settlement is final upon the close of each 30-minute epoch.

## 5. Privacy
We use Geo-IP detection to ensure legal compliance. This data is processed at the edge and not stored permanently in user profiles.
